Object 2024

The boundary between shooting and editing is fading.

As the demand for high-quality results rises, users increasingly seek casual, effortless workflows — and real-time editing emerges as the answer.

Preseter brings the app’s real-time settings interface into a physical device, letting you edit, adjust, and apply presets while shooting.

Key elements like exposure, luminance, and contrast are controlled through intuitive knob sliders.

You can also save and recall your own presets via wheel scrolling, with Bluetooth support for smartphone lenses like ultra-wide and telephoto. Preseter redefines the photography experience for a new generation.
